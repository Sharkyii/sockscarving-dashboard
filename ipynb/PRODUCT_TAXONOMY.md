# Product Taxonomy: Product Families & Style Tags

This dashboard derives two extra fields for every line item on top of the raw `Lineitem name`, computed in `modules/data_processing.py` (`_get_product_family` / `_get_style_tags`):

- **Product Family** -- the raw `Category` (everything before "EDITION", or before the first " - ") with leading/trailing color & pattern descriptors stripped, so color variants of the same product line (e.g. `BLACK- ADDY EDITION`, `WHITE- ADDY EDITION`) collapse into one family (`ADDY EDITION`). Used for the Top Categories / Trend / Delivery-Performance charts on the Products tab.

- **Style Tags** -- a comma-separated list of cross-cutting style attributes (No-Show, Loafer, Gift, Kids, ...) detected by keyword in the `Lineitem name`. A single Product Family can carry multiple tags, and the same tag appears across many families. Used as a multiselect filter on the Products tab to slice every chart down to e.g. "all No-Show styles" regardless of which family they belong to.


**Summary:** 787 raw `Category` values -> 528 Product Families (326 categories were remapped/consolidated; the rest were already a clean family on their own).
